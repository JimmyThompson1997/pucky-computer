About

Pucky is a voice-first AI agent for your smartphone.

Pucky lives on your Android, connects to your apps, and helps turn short voice commands into useful actions.

FAQ

Quick questions

Who is it for?

Busy individuals and professionals who want a powerhouse personal digital assistant to help you run your life.

What is Pucky?

Your personal digital assistant.

Is this an Android app?

Yes it is, but it is currently in beta and only downloadable via the APK, not in the Play Store.

How do I get this?

Copy this repo URL and paste it into your Claude Code, Codex, or Gemini agent: https://github.com/JimmyThompson1997/Motorolla. Ask the agent to clone the repo and build the Android APK.

What can it connect to?

On your Android device, email, calendar, chat, tasks, files, and other on-device apps. And other popular apps like Gmail, Google Calendar, Slack, Notion, GitHub, Google Drive, Airtable, Salesforce, Dropbox, and Trello. You can see the full integration list at composio.dev.

Does it replace my apps?

No.

How do I talk to it?

Hold a button, say what you need, and let Pucky prepare the next step.

Can it run actions on my phone?

Yes, when granted permissions like SMS, Phone, Photos, Video, Contacts, etc. access Pucky can take actions related to those sections of the Android operating system.

Is it finished?

No. This page is a preview of the product direction.

Where can I follow the build?

Follow me on X.
